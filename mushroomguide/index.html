Mushroom Guide

This beautifully illustrated mushroom guide app puts a constantly growing catalog of fungi on your iPhone or iPad.

Hand drawn scientific illustrations for each species by renowned artist and avid mycophile Julie Himes.

When it comes to wild mushrooms it is always helpful to have a reliable and clear information at hand that strips away all the complexity of recognizing various types of mushrooms growing in North America.

50+ species of mushrooms growing in North America. This number is constantly growing as our team adds more and more with every update.
