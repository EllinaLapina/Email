Your trip to St. John is coming up soon! Here are some helpful tips for traveling to the island.

Departure

Email our concierge, Jennifer, with your St. Thomas flight info and let her know of any changes along the way.

Arrival

Once you land on St. Thomas you will need to get a shuttle to either Red Hook (40 min ride) or Charlotte Amalie (10 min ride). Both the shuttle and the ferry are cash only. There is an ATM at the airport if needed. Once you’re on the shuttle, give Jennifer a call at 340-998-0006 and let her know what ferry you hope to catch. We have included the ferry schedule below.
